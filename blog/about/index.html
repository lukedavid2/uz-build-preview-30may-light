About the writers

A short note on who writes Field Notes, and why we are open about it.

Field Notes is written by three named writers: Marcus Vale, June Okafor, and Sasha Reyes. They are personas. The voices are carefully designed and consistent, but the writers are not real people. We disclose this because transparency matters more than pretending otherwise. The tools remain the main event. If the distinction matters to you, fair enough. The work is still here, free.
